PRESS RELEASE:

Canterbury of New Zealand is pleased to announce a new partnership with The Rugby Breakdown.

"We are delighted to announce this new partnership with The Rugby Breakdown. Editor-in-chief Jackie Fintan has been doing a fine job covering women's rugby of all competetive levels across the United States. We aim to support Jackie's committment and vision of increasing the exposure and coverage of women's rugby and providing useful online resources for those who follow or involved in women's rugby. Jackie has already published a wide range of quality rugby news and content and we are excited to support the The Rugby Breakdown with new initiatives through 2017 and beyond" - Commented Robbie Flynn. CEO Canterbury North America.

"I'm excited to align with a company that is so prevalently invested in the growth of U.S. rugby: said Jackie Finlan. editor-in-chief of The Rugby Breakdown. -The site produces news specific to the women's game. all in the hopes of developing a well informed. connected constituency. Canterbury's support will deepen and enrich the site's reach. and that's invaluable. especially as The Rugby Breakdown moves into its first fall season of coverage."

Canterbury supports additional rugby news entities in the states. but this is the first female-oriented outlet to join its lineup. The partnership comes on the heels of USA Women's 7's team fifth-place finish at the Olympics: the continual addition of NCAA varsity programs: increasing number of high school and collegiate All-American fixtures: and the general uptick in player participation.

About The Rugby Breakdown

Launched in January 2016 by veteran rugby reporter Jackie Finlan. the site produces original news on the women's game in the United States. Reportage ranges from girls' high schools to the national teams, and everything in between. The goal is to not only grow women's rugby but also showcase the athletes. teams and coaches who deserve attention.
